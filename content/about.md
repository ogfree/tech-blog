# About Techra

Techra is an independent technology publication focused on **news, startups, software, and the evolving digital landscape**.

The goal of Techra is simple:  
to track what’s changing in technology and explain *why it matters* — without hype, noise, or clickbait.

## What We Cover
- Technology news & product launches
- Startups and platforms
- Software, tools, and ecosystems
- Trends shaping the future of tech

## Editorial Approach
Techra values clarity over speed, insight over virality, and substance over sensationalism.

Every article aims to be:
- Clear and factual
- Context-aware
- Useful to builders, learners, and curious readers

## About the Author
Techra is written and maintained by **Shyam**, a technologist and writer exploring software, systems, and modern technology.
