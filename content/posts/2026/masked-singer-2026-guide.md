## What is The Masked Singer?

For those new to the phenomenon, *The Masked Singer* is a reality singing competition where celebrities perform in elaborate costumes, concealing their identities. A panel of judges and the audience must guess who is behind the mask based on performance, voice, and cryptic clues revealed in packages.

### Why It’s a Global Hit
- **The Mystery:** The core appeal is the "whodunit" element.
- **The Spectacle:** Costumes are becoming more intricate and technological every year.
- **The Reveal:** The moment the mask comes off is often a viral sensation.
